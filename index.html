About Nothing SMP

Nothing SMP is a lightly modded Survival Multiplayer server where players can build, explore, and thrive in a friendly community. With fair play as our core, fights occur only for valid reasons. Enjoy custom mods, an economy system, regular events, and more with our 20 dedicated players!

Server Features

Lightly Modded

Vanilla-like gameplay with quality-of-life mods for an enhanced experience.

Fair PvP

Fights only for valid reasons—no griefing or random attacks.

Economy & Events

Player-driven economy with weekly events and custom builds.
