About Nothing SMP

Nothing SMP is a premier lightly modded Survival Multiplayer server, designed for players to build, explore, and thrive in a welcoming community. With a commitment to fair play, battles are reserved for valid reasons. Enjoy custom mods, a robust economy, regular events, and more with our dedicated 20-player community.
