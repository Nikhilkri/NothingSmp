About Nothing SMP

Nothing SMP is a lightly modded Survival Multiplayer server where players can build, explore, and interact in a friendly environment. With a focus on fair play, fights only occur for valid reasons, promoting a positive community. Our server features custom mods for enhanced gameplay, economy systems, regular events, and more. Join our 20 dedicated players in this blocky adventure!
